Eigenvalues
The eigenvalues for both the interval and SG are in csv files.

For the threshold division scheme, the cutoff values are determined in the following way. Cutoff 1 is always 0, i.e. the standard division scheme. For the remaining 4, we take the two measure parameters, and choose 4 evenly spaced points between the values, excluding the smaller measure parameter and including the larger measure parameter. They are numbered in increasing order.
